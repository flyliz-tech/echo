# Governance

## Project Model

Echo follows a maintainer-led model.

- Maintainers steward roadmap, releases, and final merge decisions.
- Contributors propose changes through issues and pull requests.
- Significant technical changes should be discussed in an issue before a large PR.

## Decision Making

- Small changes: maintainer review and merge.
- Breaking or architectural changes: maintainer consensus after community discussion.
- If consensus is unclear, maintainers decide and document rationale in the related issue or PR.

## Maintainers

Current maintainers and ownership are listed in [MAINTAINERS.md](MAINTAINERS.md).

## Contribution Expectations

- Be respectful and follow [CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md).
- Keep PRs focused and testable.
- Follow [CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md) for branch, commit, and review process.

## Conflict Resolution

For disputes on direction or behavior:

1. Discuss in the relevant issue/PR with technical context.
2. If unresolved, request maintainer mediation.
3. For conduct issues, follow reporting in [CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md).
